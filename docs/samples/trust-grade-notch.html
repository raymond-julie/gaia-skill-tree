Trust Grade Notch

I8 · 4 grades × 6 plaque variants / dev/review artifact

The Trust Grade notch is a small rectangular metallic corner stamp that appears at the bottom-right of every .plaque variant. It shows the skill's overallTrustGrade field: S (Platinum), A (Gold), B (Silver), C (Bronze). Ungraded skills show nothing; zero markup cost when absent. This notch is distinct from the star-rank system; both coexist on the same card.

prefers-reduced-motion note: Grade S (Platinum) animates a background-position shimmer via @keyframes trust-notch-shimmer.
