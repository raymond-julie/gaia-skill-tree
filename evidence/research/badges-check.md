Why might my badge appear blank in my README?


If your repo isn’t registered yet, the badge stays
_“validating…”_ for up to 24h while caches roll. Load
the badge URL in a new tab to confirm it’s live — if you see it
there, only GitHub’s cache is stale.

## The rank system

Every badge panel color maps to a rank. Here is what each rank means — earned through evidence, never declared.

| Stars | Rank | Color | Evidence floor | What it takes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0★ | Unawakened | Slate | None | Generic primitive — catalogued but not yet verified by any implementation. |
| 1★ | Awakened | Sky blue | None | Verified as a real, distinct capability. First sighting confirmed. |
| 2★ | Named | Teal | Class C | Minimum rank for a Named Skill. A contributor's handle goes on the registry permanently at this threshold. |
| 3★ | Evolved | Violet | Class B | Reproducible and stable. The implementation can be demonstrated consistently, with docs and a public repo link required. |
| 4★ | Hardened | Fuchsia | Class B/A | Production-ready. Comprehensive tests, active maintenance, and documented reliability in real-world usage. |
| 4★+ | ◉ Unique | Deep violet | Class B/A | A separate tier — not a rank step. A Basic Skill that reached elite mastery through depth alone, without ever fusing. Graph-isolated, no fusion path forward. Badge renders in deep violet on a black panel. |
| 5★ | Transcendent | Amber | Class B/A + 10k★ | Mastery. Often an Ultimate capstone. Requires a repository with 10,000+ stars and peer-reviewed or battle-tested evidence. |
| 6★ | Apex | Gold → Cyan | Class A + Origin 5★ Fusion | The pinnacle of Gaia. Extreme ecosystem impact. Must emerge from a fusion involving at least one Origin 5★ Transcendent skill, with Tier A evidence. |

## What your badge means

Every badge tells a specific story. Here's what each one signals to anyone who reads your README.

| Badge | What it signals | Who earns it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `handle.svg` | Your identity as a named contributor — `@handle` in honor red, your top skill in its rank color, star count alongside. The full picture at a glance. | Contributors with at least one named skill in the registry. |
| `rank.svg` | Your highest earned rank (1★ Awakened → 6★ Apex). Color-coded by tier — cyan at 1★, gold at 5–6★. Regenerates on every release so it always reflects your current standing. | Anyone with a promoted skill in their tree. |
| `skills.svg` | Total named skills you hold in the canonical registry. A measure of breadth — how many distinct capabilities you've proven, reviewed, and shipped. | Contributors with multiple named skills. |
| `/skill-slug.svg` | A badge for a specific named skill — same format as `handle.svg` but pinned to one capability. Use it in the repo where that skill lives. | Named contributors showcasing a single skill in context. |

Badges are static SVGs regenerated by `gaia docs build` and served from
`gaia.tiongson.co`. GitHub's camo proxy caches images for ~24 hours, so newly earned
ranks may take a day to propagate after a release. Logo: the Gaia diamond seal.
