# GAIA Strategic Roadmap

## Vision
GAIA becomes the canonical reputation, verification, and discovery layer for AI-agent capabilities and SKILL.md ecosystems.

## North Star
When developers, researchers, recruiters, or organizations ask:
- Who is best at X?
- Which implementation is trusted?
- Which repository should I use?
- Who first discovered this capability?

The answer should come from GAIA.

---

# Strategic Principles

## Evidence Over Popularity
Trust must be earned through evidence, reproducibility, and transparent scoring.

## Identity Matters
Contributors should build persistent reputation through attributable contributions.

## Rankings Must Be Explainable
Every score should have a clear explanation and improvement path.

## Discovery Must Be Continuous
Continuously surface new skills, repositories, contributors, and milestones.
