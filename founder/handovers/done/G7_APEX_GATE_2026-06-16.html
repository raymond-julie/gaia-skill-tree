The Gate

How a skill earns each star tier in the GAIA registry. Six floors of trust, one apex with nine locks. Read once, decide later.

System-wide 6★ count at G7+0: 0 of 5
0★ Basic no evidence floor

The skill exists in the canonical graph — recognised by the registry but not yet attached to anyone's tree.

1★ Awakened no evidence floor

A user has been observed practising it — gaia scan detected the skill in their work and added it to their tree.

2★ Named ≥1 evidence row at C+

The skill carries at least one piece of recorded evidence at any grade. The first acknowledgement that the skill is real and demonstrable.

3★ Evolved ≥1 evidence row at B+

At least one B-grade row. A meaningful endorsement — not just a self-attestation, but a real signal that an outside observer would recognise.

4★ Hardened ≥1 evidence row at B+ · rank-floor protected

Same evidence floor as 3★, with one teeth-bared rule: cannot land below B without explicit PR review (§10.10). Once you cross 4★, you don't accidentally fall back to C.

5★ Transcendent Overall Trust Grade ≥ B · rank-floor protected

A skill in serious standing. Typically a suite Ultimate or a deeply-evidenced single skill. Rank-floor still protects against accidental sub-B drops.

  • Multiple evidence types preferred but not required.
  • This is the bar most contributors aim for. The gap from here to 6★ is intentionally large.
6★ Apex

Nine locks. Every one must open. The tier is structurally rare — fewer than five at any moment, system-wide. The promotion PR is the door; two independent verifiers from two different GitHub orgs are the keys.

⚙≥12 nested origins
Fusion-recipe origins counted across the full transitive tree of suiteComponents, deduped by skillId, graded ≥ C. Forces the gate past the sqrt-softening knee.
⛓Direct nest
At least one direct component must itself be a suite/Ultimate. Apex stacks on top of 5★ infrastructure — it doesn't sit beside it.
⤷Real depth
At least one node in the closure must be reachable only through nesting — not also a direct child. Blocks the flat-pancake failure mode.
◆Grade S, no phantoms
Trust Magnitude ≥ 250 from rows that physically exist in the skill's evidence: array. Auto-mint applies only to fusion-recipe.
✦≥8 A-graded
At least 8 distinct skills in the closure must hold Overall Trust Grade ≥ A. No riding sqrt-softening on a B-graded substrate.
⚐≥2 cross-org cosigns
Two 4★+ verifier-attestations from two distinct GitHub orgs. Single-mothership skills cannot self-attest to apex.
⏳≥180 days tenure
Earliest evidence row at least 180 calendar days old. Aligned with the 6-month provisional-grade grace window.
⊛PR-gated promotion
gaia promote rejects 6★. Apex requires a PR labelled apex-promotion with ≥2 verifier sign-offs ticking each predicate.
#Cap of 5
No more than 5 apex skills system-wide at any moment. A sixth landing requires an existing apex to demote — or an RFC amendment.

Five things to remember

  1. Stars are earned by evidence, not by claim. The number on the badge is the floor on what your evidence already supports.
  2. Grades stack; tiers don't. A row at S anchors S; you can't average four C rows into a B.
  3. Phantom rows don't count. If it's not in the skill's evidence: array, it contributes zero. (§10.14 — registry-wide, not just at apex.)
  4. 6★ is structurally rare. Fewer than five at any moment. No auto-promote. Two independent verifiers from two different GitHub orgs must sign a PR.
  5. Demotion is honest. At G7 cutover, both standing 6★ skills demote to 5★ because they fail the new gate. Re-application is open immediately; the bar is just higher.

What changes at G7 cutover

mattpocock/skills and ruvnet/ruflo — the two skills currently sitting at 6★ — both demote to 5★ in the migration PR. Neither carries the cross-org verifier cosigns or the evidence-type diversity the new gate demands. Both land at 5★ Transcendent with grade A provisional.

Both can re-apply immediately via the PR-gated path. The door is open. The bar is just higher.
