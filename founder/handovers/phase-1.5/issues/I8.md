## Objective

Add a **Trust Grade notch** — a small rectangular metallic corner stamp — to every `.plaque` variant across the site. The notch shows the skill's `overallTrustGrade` (S/A/B/C = Platinum/Gold/Silver/Bronze). Ungraded skills show nothing.

This is the evidence record made visible at a glance: subtle but proud, like a hallmark stamp on silverware. **Distinct** from the star-rank system. Strictly boxy/rectangular — no circular shapes, no new glyph families.

---

## Design language

### Placement

Bottom-right corner of every `.plaque`. Sits inside the card boundary (not overflowing), flush with bottom and right edges. The card's `border-radius` clips the notch corner so it reads as a corner-stamped mark.

```
+------------------------------+
|  ◆ mattpocock/skills         |
|  5★ Transcendent             |
|  Matt Pocock · …             |
|                          [A] |  <- Gold notch, bottom-right
+------------------------------+
```

The notch contains in `var(--font-mono)`:
- Grade letter (S/A/B/C) at ~0.7rem bold
- Grade name (Platinum/Gold/Silver/Bronze) at ~0.6rem — **hidden on `.plaque--mini` and `.plaque--row`**

### Grade materials

| Grade | Fill | Text color |
|---|---|---|
| S — Platinum | Iridescent titanium `--grade-S` base, radial highlight, **animated diagonal shimmer sweep** (3.5s loop). `prefers-reduced-motion`: static metallic, no sweep. | `#0b2545` |
| A — Gold | `var(--grade-A)` base, fine linear brush, static polished reflection | `#451a03` |
| B — Silver | Dark steel `--grade-B` base, coarser matte reflection | `#0f172a` |
| C — Bronze | `var(--grade-C)` base, rough linear, tarnished | `#fffbeb` |

**No hex literals in new CSS.** Use `--grade-S / --grade-A / --grade-B / --grade-C` tokens from `docs/css/tokens.css`.

### Ungraded

Omit the notch element entirely. No ghost bar. (~235 of 235 skills currently ungraded; notch appears once evidence is ingested.)

## Constraints

- No hex literals in new CSS.
- No `border-left` > 1px colored accent (DESIGN.md absolute ban).
- No circular shapes or `border-radius` on the notch face itself (the card's `border-radius` clips the corner — do not add a separate radius to `.plaque__trust-notch`).
- `prefers-reduced-motion`: Platinum shimmer reduces to static metallic gradient.
- WCAG AA: all grade text meets ≥4.5:1 against metallic fill.
- Do NOT modify `pyproject.toml`, `packages/cli-npm/package.json`, `packages/mcp/package.json`, `registry/gaia.json`.
- Branch: `design/trust-grade-notch`. Label: `skip-scope-check`.
- **Do NOT touch Hermes-owned files:** `STEWARDSHIP_PLAN.md`, `scripts/marketing_engine.py`, `scripts/email_sender.py`, `scripts/share_deliverable.py`, `scripts/generate_adoption_dashboard.py`, `scripts/generate_showcase.py`, `docs/ADOPTION.html`, `docs/SHOWCASE.html`, `docs/WHY-GAIA.md`, `docs/QUICKSTART.md`.

## What this is NOT

- Not a replacement for the star-rank system. Both coexist.
- Not a circular shape.
- Not a `data-rarity` attribute (rarity deprecated per `CONTEXT.md`).
- Not a glow or color-cycling effect.
