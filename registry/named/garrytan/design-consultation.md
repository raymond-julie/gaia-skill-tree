## Overview

Gstack Design Consultation builds your design system from the ground up rather than extracting an existing one. It analyses the product's purpose and target users, benchmarks competitors, then proposes a unified visual language covering typography, colour palette, spacing scale, layout grid, and motion principles — written to DESIGN.md as the canonical design source of truth.
